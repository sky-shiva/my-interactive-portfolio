About Me

Hey, I’m Shiva — a data science enthusiast who loves building things that actually solve problems, not just sit in a GitHub repo gathering dust.

I’m in my B.Tech journey, constantly tinkering with AI, analytics, and full-stack development like they’re puzzle pieces waiting to click. I’m the kind of person who asks “why?” a little too often, breaks things just to understand them, and then builds them back cleaner.

"Data, to me, is a story. Code is the pen. And I’m just trying to write something worth reading."

When I’m not debugging or wrestling with Git, I’m probably learning something new, planning my next project, or imagining tools that make people’s lives 1% easier.

This is me — curious, hands-on, slightly stubborn, and always building.
